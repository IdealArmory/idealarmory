About Us

Ideal Armory was built by gun enthusiasts, for gun enthusiasts. We know the feeling of spending hours across a dozen browser tabs trying to find the best price on a rifle, optic, or that last part to complete a build — so we built the tool we always wished existed. Ideal Armory brings firearms, ammunition, optics, and accessory pricing from across the web into one place, so you can spend less time searching and more time at the range or out in the field.

We’re more than a price comparison tool. Our Kit Builder lets you design your ideal setup from top to bottom. Whether you’re looking for your first pistol, or building out a precision long-range rig, you can find the best deal, all in one place. We also include a built-in FFL Finder to help you locate a licensed dealer near you.

We’re big believers in supporting the full ecosystem of the firearms community. We want to show you the best deals from the local mom and pop shops all the way up to the big box stores. We hope you enjoy spending time on our platform.
